# HERVITTA  
### Voice-Assisted Financial Planning for Women

“We are not just tracking money — we are building financial confidence.”

## 🚀 Problem
Many women in India lack structured financial guidance, awareness of government schemes, and confidence in managing money.  
Existing apps focus on transactions, not understanding.

---

## 💡 Solution
**HERVITTA** is a simple, voice-assisted prototype that helps women:
- Turn financial goals into actionable plans  
- Learn through voice and video guidance  
- Access relevant financial schemes easily  

---

## ✨ Key Features
- 🎯 **Goal-Based Savings Planner**  
  Convert savings goals into monthly plans instantly  

- 🎤 **Voice Guidance (Simulated)**  
  Easy-to-understand audio support for better accessibility  

- 🎥 **Video Learning Support**  
  Short videos to guide users on financial decisions  

- 📜 **Scheme Recommendations**  
  Suggests relevant government schemes  

- 📊 **Simple & Clear Interface**  
  Designed for all users — students, homemakers, and professionals  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Antigravity 
  
---

## 📱 How It Works
1. User enters a savings goal and time duration  
2. App calculates required monthly savings  
3. Voice/video guidance helps user understand the plan  
4. Relevant schemes are suggested
